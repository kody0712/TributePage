Billy The Kid

The Troubled Life

The time line of Billy the Kid's life.

  • Born to the name Henry McCarty on September 17 or November 23, 1859 in New York City.
  • Orphaned at the age of 15.
  • First arrest was for stealing food at 16.
  • After murdering a blacksmith in 1877 Billy became a wanted man in Arizona
  • Sheriff Pat Garrett captured Billy the kid in December of 1880.
  • Billy would be convicted for the murder of Sheriff Brady of Lincoln County and his deputy.
  • Billy escaped from jail on April 1881.
  • Sheriff Garrett would eventually catch up to Billy the Kid on July 14, 1881 and shoot him.
  • Legends grew that Billy the Kid survived the shooting and numerous men claimed to be him.
